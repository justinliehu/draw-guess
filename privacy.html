Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

This app ("Draw & Guess" / Party Games) is a multiplayer game. We respect your privacy.

Data we use

  • In-game: Your chosen display name, room ID, and game actions (drawing, guesses, scores) are stored temporarily on our game server to run the game. This data is not sold.
  • Wallet: If you connect a wallet (e.g. Phantom, Solflare), we do not collect or store your wallet address on our own servers beyond what is needed to show your name in the room. Connection is handled by your wallet extension.
  • Local storage: We may store preferences (e.g. language, last room) in your browser to improve your experience.

Third parties

We use a game server to sync rooms. We do not share your data with advertisers or third parties for marketing.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact us via the app website or repository.
